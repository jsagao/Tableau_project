## üìà Project Insights Summary

This project demonstrates applied data analytics and visualization using **Tableau Desktop**, focusing on **sales performance, profitability, and regional business trends**. It integrates over **100 calculated fields** to generate automated KPIs, predictive forecasts, and interactive visuals for executive decision support.

### üîπ Key Features
- **KPI Automation:** Month-over-month revenue growth, moving averages, and profit-margin calculations dynamically update from source data.  
- **Predictive Modeling:** Dual-axis trend analysis combines actual revenue with forecasted projections.  
- **Geospatial Analytics:** Interactive map and treemap visuals reveal regional revenue concentration and store-level performance.  
- **Comparative Dashboards:** Market share, category breakdowns, and regional comparisons highlight areas of opportunity.  

### üîπ Tools & Techniques
- **Tableau Desktop** for data visualization, dashboard design, and storytelling.  
- **Calculated Fields & Parameters** for custom KPI creation and user interactivity.  
- **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing** performed externally (Python/pandas).  
- **Predictive & Statistical Functions** for forecasting, trend smoothing, and outlier detection.

### üîπ Outcome
A professional-grade Tableau dashboard enabling:
- Rapid insights into **sales and profitability performance**  
- **Data-driven strategic decisions** across time and geography  
- Seamless translation of raw datasets into **actionable business intelligence**

üìå *Keywords:* Tableau, Data Visualization, KPI Dashboard, Predictive Analytics, Business Intelligence, Data Storytelling, Python, Forecast Modeling.
